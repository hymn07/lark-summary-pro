Welcome to the Flowmail documentation. Here you'll find everything you need to get started and make the most of the platform.

## What is Flowmail?

Flowmail is an **AI-native business execution platform** that sits on top of your email inbox. It automatically classifies incoming emails, extracts structured business data, and enables you to take action — all without leaving a single interface.

## Core Concepts

- **Smart Inbox** — Your emails organized by business type: Approvals, Reports, Issues, and more
- **Entities** — Structured business items extracted from emails with full timeline tracking
- **Actions** — One-click operations: approve, reject, update status, assign, and follow up
- **AI Pipeline** — Automatic classification and data extraction with human-in-the-loop confirmation

## Getting Started

1. [Create your account](/docs/getting-started/overview) and set up your organization
2. Connect your email (Gmail or Outlook) via secure OAuth
3. Start processing emails — Flowmail's AI will classify and extract data automatically
4. Review AI suggestions and take action

## Scenarios

### Approval Workflows
Turn approval request emails into trackable items. See who's asking, what they need, and when it's due. Approve or reject with a single click.

### Report Tracking
Automatically extract progress updates from team reports. Track status changes over time and get alerted when risks are detected.

## Architecture

Flowmail exposes two API layers for maximum flexibility:

- **oRPC** — Type-safe RPC for the web UI (used by the dashboard)
- **MCP Server** — Model Context Protocol for AI agent integration (used by Claude, GPT, Cursor, and other agents)

Both layers share the same core business logic, ensuring consistency across all interfaces.
